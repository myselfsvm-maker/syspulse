Device Info
📱 IMEI & Device Identity
NOT ACCESSIBLE VIA BROWSER
🔒 IMEI cannot be read by any browser or web app. Android blocked JS access to IMEI from Android 7.0+ for privacy. Requires a native app with READ_PHONE_STATE permission — and Google Play restricts IMEI collection strictly.
✅ Use one of the 4 methods below to find your IMEI instantly.
How to Find Your IMEI
1Dial Code — Fastest Method
Open your Phone app and dial *#06# — IMEI appears immediately. Works on all Android phones without any app or settings.
2Android Settings
Settings → About Phone → Status → IMEI Info
Some phones: Settings → About → SIM Status
3Physical SIM Tray / Box
IMEI is printed on the SIM tray, back cover sticker, or the original retail box of your phone.
4Google Account
Visit myaccount.google.com → Security → Your Devices → tap your phone to view its IMEI registered with Google.
